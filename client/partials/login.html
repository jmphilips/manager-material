Manager Material

Manager Material is an app that allows a manager to manage different projects on a team.

When a manager goes to the home page, the manager can see an overview of current projects, employees, and a calendar displaying all of the projects.

Slack Integration gives employees the ability to quickly and easily view and update projects via slack.

Automated Emails keeps customers in the loop without disrupting employee workflow.

Calendar Overview shows managers the big picture about what their teams are working on.
